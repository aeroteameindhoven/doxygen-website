void calibrate ( )

Calibrates the lift motors by moving them up until the respective calibration buttons are pressed. Once a button is pressed, the corresponding motor holds its position. After both motors are calibrated, it moves the motors back down to the default positions. This ensures that the system has a known reference point for future movements.

◆ sequence()

void sequence ( )

Full sequence of moving the battery down, switching sides and moving it up again.

The sequence consists of the following steps:

  1. Move the battery down.
  2. Switch the lock state of the top side.
  3. Rotate to the opposite side.
  4. Switch the lock state of the new top side.
  5. Move the battery up.
